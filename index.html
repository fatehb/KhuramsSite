About us

Who We Are

Tera Consulting is a New York State, New York City, Nassau County, & Port Authority certified Minority Woman Owned Business Enterprise (M/WBE). Tera Consulting started as a Marketing and Sales consulting firm and has expanded to provide paper products, office furniture, and office supplies to local, state, and federal agencies as well as private corporations in the tri-state New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut areas. We are your one-stop source for everything you need to help your business operate efficiently and improve your bottom line.

We have a culture of Total Performance; a commitment to the highest standards in every aspect of the way we do business.

  • Nassau County M/WBE

  • Woman Owned Small Business (WOSB)

Why Choose Us

We are a certified Minority Woman Owned Business Enterprise operating out of Nassau County Long Island. Established in 2011, Tera Consulting, Inc. takes pride in providing high quality paper products, office products, and office furniture at the best prices to government, state, and city agencies, as well as corporate offices.

Our culture focuses not on what we do, but on how we do it. If we focus on the how, the future that we wish to attain will follow.
